Findings

Unearned Blur HIGH

20+ tiers of glassmorphism. Every component uses backdrop-blur-2xl, blue-tinted borders, top highlight streaks, and ambient blue gradients. The question "does this surface need blur to communicate its role?" was never asked. Post cards, suggestion cards, context banners, buttons, badges, and dividers all get the same frosted treatment.

Reflex: Glassmorphism was chosen as a blanket visual system, not as a deliberate accent. Blur does not help the user compare profiles, monitor activity, or find collaborators — it adds visual weight to everything equally, erasing hierarchy.

Generic Tech Hue HIGH

Brand color oklch(0.488 0.243 264.376) is a purple-blue. Every accent follows: blue-400/10 borders, blue-950/0.05 backgrounds, blue-300/30 highlights. The entire color system is a single blue family — the default "tech collaboration platform" palette.

Reflex: Blue-purple signals nothing specific about Collabryx. It could be any SaaS, any startup, any developer tool. A collaboration platform for founders needs warmth, trust, and energy — not corporate safety.

Feature Tile Grid MEDIUM

All posts render as identical PostCard components — same padding, same glass, same structure. The suggestions sidebar has three equal match cards. When every item is equally important, nothing is prioritized.

Reflex: The layout comes from the loop (.map()) rather than editorial judgment. Pinned or relevant content should look different.

Stat Monument MEDIUM

Match percentage is displayed at text-2xl font-bold, dominating the card. The person's name, role, and reasons take secondary visual weight below the number.

Reflex: The number is treated as the primary evidence because it is easy to compute, but a percentage alone is not the evidence the user needs to act on a match.

Icon Topper LOW

Sparkles appears on the AI Context Card, Smart Matches header, and embedding banner. Bot on the AI Mentor card. Inbox on empty states. Every section heading is topped with a rounded icon that carries no information the heading does not already convey.

Reflex: Icons are being used to "decorate" section headers — a common template pattern. Remove them; keep icons only for functional states.

Accent Rail LOW

Unread notifications get border-l-4 border-l-primary — a colored stripe duplicating the unread dot. Adds no information; decoration dressed as organization.

Bounce Everywhere LOW

Glass cards have transition-all duration-500 ease-in-out and hover lift. Applied globally, this creates a sluggish feel. Content cards should respond in 200-300ms; 400-500ms is for entrance animations only.
